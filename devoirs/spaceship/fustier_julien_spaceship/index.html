Welcome to my game:

Steel Ball Run


Rule of the game:

The concept is very simple: This is an individual race game. You have to finish the race as faster as you can.


Your time will be registered so the chalenge is to beat your previously highscore. Make your best to make a good time and of course, i hope you will have a good time. Good Game!


Control


Press key:a to start the game

  • Arrow Up: acelerate
  • Arrow Down: decelerate
  • Arrow Left: move left
  • Arrow Right: move right
